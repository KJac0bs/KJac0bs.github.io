Our Services

Keeping your pool clean is very important and our technicians know how to quickly and effectively clean your pools. We provide the following pool cleaning services:

  • Once off pool cleaning
  • Weekly scheduled pool cleaning
  • Chemical balancing
  • Filter sand changing

We provide maintenance to ensure a safe swimming environment. We provide the following pool maintenance services:

  • Weekly pool backwashing and rinsing
  • Regular testing & correcting of pool acid levels
  • Weekly pool chemical treatment

Our skilled technicians will identify your pools problem and fix it. We provide the following repair services:

  • Pump, filter, and, heater repairs
  • Pipe leakage indentification and repair
